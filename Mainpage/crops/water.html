Procedures to Grow Watermelons

Step 1: Preparation

Prepare the soil for planting. Watermelons prefer a well-drained soil rich in organic matter.

Step 2: Planting

Plant the watermelon seeds in the soil, during the spring season. Make sure to leave enough space between each seed as watermelon plants can grow quite large.

Step 3: Watering

Water regularly but do not overwater. Watermelons prefer slightly dry conditions.

Step 4: Harvesting

Harvest the watermelons when the fruits are fully ripe and have a deep, rich color.
